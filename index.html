AR-Alpha

Kids learn the most by visualisation and interaction and it is difficult to replicate that in a traditional way of teaching. We can overcome the challenges posed by the traditional teaching methods using Ar-Alpha: An Augmented Reality based application which engages the children and lets them have fun and learn new concepts along the way. Using AR, 3D objects of words that begin with each alphabet can be seen and children can interact with it. Game-based quiz and A portal classroom door lets them have fun and test their understanding.

AI Based Realtime Sudoku Solver

Everyone must be aware of the Sudoku game (a logic-based combinatorial number-placement puzzle). With the help of computervision using python3 i made a realtime solver which takes the grid from an image shown through the webcam of the PC/laptop and with the help classification model using neuralnetworks trained on my dataset with resnet50 using Fastai extracted the content from the image and solved the game with Backtracking Algorithm. The final output is the solved sudoku with all the previously empty spaces filled with the desired number for a successful Sudoku game.

AgroPro

Agriculture is one of the important industrial sectors in India and the country’s economy is highly dependent on it for rural sustainability. Due to some factors like climate changes, unpredicted rainfall, decrease of water level, lack of knowledge of governmental schemes, etc. the level of agriculture in India has decreased. AgroPro provides a system which predicts the annual and monthly rainfall for Madhya Maharashtra, gives information on crops on the basis of region’s rainfall and type of soil. The application also contains live news feed related to rainfall, agriculture, farming and irrigation and also have information about government schemes related to agriculture.
